About Me

Aliyu Aliyu Alkali, popularly known as Pantami Jr, is a digital transformation enthusiast and the Managing Director of the Isa Ali Pantami Bilingual & Digital Academy. He served as the Kebbi State Representative of the Illmi Children's Fund. Additionally, he is the founder of the A A Alkali Foundation, which is dedicated to enhancing education opportunities for youth and providing learning alternatives to vulnerable youths in his community.

Alkali holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Economics and Public Policy from Hassan II University of Casablanca, Morocco, and is pursuing a degree in computer science at Maryam Abacha American University of Niger. He also earned an Academic Diploma in Computer Science from Adamu Augie College of Education, with a GPA of 3.31, and a Certificate of language in French from Universit√© Mohammed V de Rabat, Morocco.

Furthermore, Alkali holds certificates in Professional Public Speaking from the African Leadership Institute and in Project Management from DEXA. He has actively participated in national and international conferences, including GITEX Africa in Marrakech, Morocco, and the Youniverse Business Summit in Bengurier. Alkali has interned at both the Nigerian Communications Commission and the Nigerian Midstream and Downstream Petroleum Regulatory Authority.

With proficiency in English, French, Fulani, Hausa, and intermediate Arabic, Alkali is multilingual and has traveled to six countries. He is a Member of the Nigerian Computer Society and a versatile individual.
